ðŸ¦  About

Covid-19, short for "coronavirus disease 2019", is the new respiratory disease spreading around the world. The virus is thought to spread mainly between people who are in close contact with one another and through respiratory droplets produced when an infected person coughs or sneezes. People are most contagious when they are the sickest. But those who don't have a lot of symptoms (asymptomatic) people can still pass the virus to others. Currently there is no vaccine or cure for Covid-19.

ðŸ¤’ Symptoms

The most common symptoms are very similar to other viruses: fever, cough, and difficulty breathing. Symptoms may appear 2 to 14 days after exposure and range from mild to severe illness.
